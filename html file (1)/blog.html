Influence of technology on daily life and business practices As the adage goes, necessity is the mother of invention. All technologies that have emerged and thrived till now have been eventually invented just to fulfill societal needs. And thus, in today’s world, technology and societies are intertwined by forming a dynamic cycle where each profoundly impacts the other. Advanced and modern technologies have emerged to address the needs, requirements or desires of evolving humans in the world.


Throughout the tapestry of history, profound changes have occurred in societies as a result of technological advancements. Technology has ultimately altered human lives, leading to radical differences compared to earlier decades. This has played a significant role in determining human behaviors and interactions with the world. There is the presence and influence of technology in various aspects of daily lives like in the field of education, health, agriculture, transportation, and entertainment. So, there is no doubt that human lives have been uplifted and overwhelmed by the emergence of technological advancements. Moreover, the “Internet” has revolutionized daily life, making several aspects a lot more convenient and accessible.


Technology acts as a transformative force which brings numerous advantages in business practices as well. It is constantly redefining how organizations operate, being more visible and easily accessible online to the customers to provide their services effectively. This includes significant improvements in internal processes and the services they provide. Over the past decade, strategies focused on implementing digital technologies have become increasingly popular due to the benefits they bring to businesses. By embracing digital solutions, advanced tools, and technologies, organizations have efficiently managed their processes, accelerated production cycles, and enhanced product quality and customer experience. This has resulted in cost reduction, increased productivity, and improved profitability, making organizations more competitive in the market.


Altogether, the impact of technology on daily life and business practices is undeniable and substantial. It has seamlessly increased our standard of living in numerous ways, making things like communication, shopping, education, healthcare and other various aspects of life more convenient and efficient. However, alongside these transformative pros, cyber issues like cyber threats, privacy concerns, and unequal access to technology have arisen. Thus, we must use and follow all the security guidelines while enjoying and surfing the online platforms.
